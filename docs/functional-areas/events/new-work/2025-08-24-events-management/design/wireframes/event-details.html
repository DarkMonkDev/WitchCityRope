Event Description

This beginner-friendly workshop introduces the fundamentals of rope bondage (shibari/kinbaku) in a safe, educational environment. You'll learn basic knots, safety principles, and simple tie patterns while building confidence and communication skills.

Perfect for complete beginners or those looking to refresh their foundation. All skill levels welcome - we'll meet you where you are and help you grow from there.

What You'll Learn:

  • Safety principles and risk awareness
  • Basic single and double column ties
  • Communication and consent practices
  • Rope handling and maintenance
  • Simple chest harness patterns

What's Included:

  • Practice rope (yours to keep)
  • Printed reference materials
  • Light refreshments
  • Ongoing community support
