About This Workshop

Join us for an comprehensive introduction to rope bondage in a welcoming, educational environment. This workshop is perfect for complete beginners who want to explore the beautiful art of rope bondage with proper technique and safety.

You'll learn fundamental knots, basic safety protocols, and simple but elegant tie patterns. We emphasize consent, communication, and creating positive experiences for both partners.

No prior experience is necessary, and all are welcome regardless of relationship status. We provide all materials and maintain a respectful, educational atmosphere throughout.

What to Bring

  • Comfortable clothing that allows for movement
  • Water bottle to stay hydrated
  • Notebook for taking notes (optional)
  • Any medical information relevant to physical activity
  • Open mind and respectful attitude

All rope and practice materials will be provided by WitchCityRope.
