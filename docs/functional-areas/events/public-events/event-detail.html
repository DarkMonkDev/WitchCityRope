Rope Basics Workshop

👥 12 person maximum

About This Class

Join us for a comprehensive introduction to Japanese rope bondage (Shibari/Kinbaku). This beginner-friendly workshop is perfect for those new to rope or looking to solidify their foundational skills.

We'll start with the basics of rope handling, safety protocols, and communication before moving into hands-on practice. You'll learn essential ties that form the building blocks of more advanced work.

What You'll Learn

  • Single column tie (the foundation of everything)
  • Double column tie variations
  • Basic chest harness (takate kote foundations)
  • Rope tension and handling techniques
  • Safety considerations and risk awareness
  • Communication and consent practices
  • How to untie safely and manage rope

What to Bring

Rope: We'll have rope available to borrow, but if you have your own, please bring 3-4 pieces of 6mm or 8mm rope (each 7-8 meters/25-30 feet long).

Partner: You can bring a practice partner or come solo - we'll help with pairing.

Other: Water bottle, comfortable clothes you can move in, and a notebook if you like to take notes.
