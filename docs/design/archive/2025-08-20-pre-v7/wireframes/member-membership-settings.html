Membership Management

Need to take a break or leave the community? You can pause your membership temporarily or withdraw permanently.

On Hold: Your profile remains active but you won't receive event invites or community emails. You can reactivate anytime.
Withdraw: Permanently removes your membership and all associated data. This action cannot be undone.

Put Membership on Hold

Putting your membership on hold will:

  • Stop all event notifications and emails
  • Hide your profile from member directory
  • Maintain your membership history and status
  • Allow you to reactivate anytime

You can reactivate your membership at any time by logging back in.

Withdraw Membership

⚠️ This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Withdrawing your membership will:

  • Permanently delete your profile and all personal data
  • Remove your access to all events and community features
  • Cancel any upcoming event registrations
  • Delete your membership history

If you want to rejoin in the future, you'll need to go through the vetting process again.
